qcmprepa is your hub for exam prep content, organized and corrected so you can focus on learning. Sign in with Google or Microsoft to get instant access to your faculty's materials and your personalized session history.

## How to sign in

- Choose **Sign in with Google** or **Sign in with Microsoft** on the home screen.
- Your account links to your faculty/year so you land directly on the right content.

## Why qcmprepa?

- **Centralized content:** Everything is organized by faculty → year → semester → module → exam/bank.
- **Quality corrections:** Each question includes curated explanations and fixes for common mistakes.
- **Smart filtering:** Jump to exactly what you need using filters by module, topic, tag, or difficulty.
