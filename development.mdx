## Exam interface

- Start an exam or MCQ bank from the module page.
- Timer, question count, and progress are visible at the top.
- Use next/previous or keyboard shortcuts to move quickly (see shortcuts page).

## Corrections

- After submitting, each question shows the correct answer and a brief correction.
- Common pitfalls are highlighted so you know what to avoid next time.
- You can revisit any question and see your original choice alongside the correction.

## Scoring

- Scores display immediately with per-question breakdowns.
- Partial credit is shown when applicable; penalties appear for negative marking when enabled.
- Your session history is saved so you can track improvement over time.
